Coeducational School

The coeducational school is committed to an educational model where gender stereotypes are eliminated and equal opportunities between boys and girls are promoted. It is not just about sharing the same space, but also about guaranteeing equitable participation, making female role models visible and promoting their co-responsibility.

Principles of coeducation

  • Eliminate gender discrimination in the classroom.
  • Promote emotional and empathetic education.
  • Include teaching materials with a gender perspective.
  • Make women leaders visible in all areas of knowledge.
  • Promote inclusive and respectful language.

Benefits for students

Implementing co-education not only improves school coexistence, but also enhances self-esteem, critical thinking and respect for differences. Students learn to value diversity as an enriching element in their education.

Co-educational activities

Some recommended activities include role dynamics, emotion workshops, gender equality debates, and media analysis. These experiences allow us to question traditional models and build new, more just ways of relating.

Committing to a co-educational school is an active contribution to social transformation. Educating for equality today is building a fairer society tomorrow. At InclusiveFundation we encourage all educational centers to integrate this perspective into their curricula and school life.
