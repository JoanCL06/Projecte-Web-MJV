Gender Equity and Coeducation

Building a fairer and more inclusive society through education

Who are we?

We are an organization committed to gender equity and educational transformation. Our team is made up of teachers, social professionals, and activists working toward inclusive education.

What do we aim to achieve?

We believe that education is a key tool for building a fairer society where everyone has equal opportunities. We aim to eliminate stereotypes, promote respect for diversity, and foster egalitarian relationships in the school environment.

Why talk about gender equity?

Gender equity seeks to eliminate structural barriers that prevent women and men from participating on equal terms in all areas of life. In education, this means reviewing content, teaching practices, and relationships within the classroom.
